## Requirements
### Requirement: App Name and Identity
The app SHALL be named "Gluten Free Baking" and branded as a comprehensive gluten-free recipe companion.

#### Scenario: App display name
- **WHEN** the app is installed on a device
- **THEN** it displays as "Gluten Free Baking" in the app launcher

#### Scenario: App header
- **WHEN** the user is on the recipe catalog screen
- **THEN** the app header displays "Gluten Free Baking" as the title

### Requirement: App Configuration
The app SHALL have updated bundle identifiers and configuration to reflect the new branding.

#### Scenario: iOS bundle identifier
- **WHEN** the app is built for iOS
- **THEN** it uses the bundle identifier "com.glutenfreebaking.app"

#### Scenario: Android package name
- **WHEN** the app is built for Android
- **THEN** it uses the package name "com.glutenfreebaking.app"

### Requirement: Gluten-Free Focus
The app SHALL emphasize its gluten-free nature in branding and descriptions.

#### Scenario: Recipe catalog subtitle
- **WHEN** viewing the recipe catalog
- **THEN** a subtitle or tagline indicates the gluten-free nature of the recipes
