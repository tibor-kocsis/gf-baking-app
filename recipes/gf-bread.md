170 g sourghum flour
130 g miklos universal
5 g psyllium husk
8 g active yeast
5 g salt
20 g oil
20 g honey
1 egg
1 table spoon lemon juice
330 g water

Instructions:

- add the yeast, lemon juice and the honey to warm water
- mix the dry ingridients
- add the egg and the luquid to the flour (except the oil)
- mix it to homogen, during mixing add the oil
- wait 15 minutes
- fold a few times the dough in layered style
- put into the pan
- score before raising
- let it raise to 120-150%
- spray the top with oil
- bake in preheated over at 170-180 C for 60 minutes
